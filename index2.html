About Dr. Rakesh Bhatt

Dr. Rakesh Bhatt is a distinguished theatre artist, director, and cultural practitioner from Uttarakhand, India. With decades of dedication to folk theatre and traditional performing arts, he has played a vital role in reviving and promoting Himalayan cultural traditions. As Director of Utsav Group, he leads productions, festivals, and youth initiatives that celebrate identity, heritage, and storytelling.
